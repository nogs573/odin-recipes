Simple Postickers - Recipe from Oli

Description

These are traditional Chinese pork dumplings. They can be customized to include whatever you want,
but this simple recipe is what I prefer. The handful of ingredients let the flavors shine through and require
very little prepwork.

Ingredients

  • 400g of ground pork
  • A bunch of chives
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 40 dumpling wrappers
  • *optional ginger

Steps

  1. Chop chives finely
  2. Mix ground pork and chives
  3. Add an egg to the mixture
  4. Mince garlic or use garlic press and add to mixture
  5. Consider adding shaved ginger here
  6. Mix until well incorporated
  7. Find a space with room and sprinkle a little bit of flour to prevent sticking
  8. Lay out a dumpling wrapper and place some mixture in the middle
  9. Use your finger to draw water around the edge of the wrapper
  10. Crimp the edges to close the dumpling, repeat until you run out of filling or wrappers
  11. To cook, place some amount of dumplings (6-8) in an oiled pan and fry on one side for 1-2 minutes
  12. Flip to the other side and fry for another minute or so
  13. Pour some water into the pan and cover to steam for 2-3 minutes
  14. Dip in a sauce of Chinese vinegar and sesame oil and enjoy!
